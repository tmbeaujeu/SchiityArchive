Website Usage Notes

  • The search bar filters products by name in real-time as you type. It is not case-sensitive and will match any part of the product name.
  • Dates are formatted as YYYY-MM-DD in order to simplify sorting.
  • Image thumbnails on a product's page can be double-clicked to immidiately show the expanded picture view.
  • You can click anywhere outside of an expanded picture's border to close the expanded view.
